# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Checklist

**Before taking any action, I will confirm the following:**

1.  **Strategic Alignment:** Does this action align with our current, primary strategic decisions? (e.g., Supabase over Firebase).
2.  **Contextual Awareness:** Have I reviewed the most recent user instructions and our `COMMON_MEMORIES.md` file to ensure I have the full picture?
3.  **Risk Mitigation:** What are the potential collateral damages or unintended consequences of this action? Have I considered a safer alternative?
4.  **Simplicity and Focus:** Is this the most direct and efficient path to the user's goal? Am I avoiding unnecessary complexity?
5.  **Confirmation:** If there is any ambiguity or risk, have I presented a clear plan and asked for user confirmation before proceeding?
